Company Overview

Kawangu Ventures Limited (Formerly Kawangu Electrical Ventures Limited) is a private limited company registered in 1982 and incorporated in 2012. The firm offers Electrical Installations and supply of electrical goods and services, Building and Constructions and Civil and Road Works. The firm also offers solutions towards the current era of result oriented and performance management and improvement. The firm offices are located within the Central Business District in Thika.
